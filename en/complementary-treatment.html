Complementary Treatment

Fillers (Juvéderm®)

The application of fillers which are Hyaluronic Acid are used to soften fine lines, wrinkles in the skin, add volume to different parts of the face and define or give volume to the lips. The results, unlike Botox®, can be appreciated immediately, giving a young, hydrated and healthy appearance to the skin. Since Hyaluronic Acid is a substance that is normally produced in the body, it is particularly safe and can be applied without risk. (approved by the FDA).

Botox®

The application of Botox® is an injectable treatment that is used aesthetically mainly on the face to eliminate wrinkles and certain lines of expression, producing relaxation of the treated muscles. The results can be seen 5 to 7 days after the application. The technique consists of injecting the product into the muscles of the areas that you want to treat with extra fine needles. The most frequent areas of application are: the eyebrows, forehead, corners of the lips and eye contour. (approved by the FDA)
