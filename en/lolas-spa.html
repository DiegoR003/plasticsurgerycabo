Featured Treatments

Explore our exclusive technology for visible results with minimal downtime.

HarmonyCa Sculptra Dermapen OxiCura Ultra Skin Booster

HarmonyCa

Dual-action collagen biostimulator combining Hyaluronic Acid (for an immediate lifting effect) with Calcium Hydroxylapatite (for long-term collagen stimulation).

Ideal for a gradual, natural tightening effect, improving skin density and firmness.

Sculptra

A powerful biostimulator that activates your body’s natural ability to produce collagen. It’s not a filler — it’s an activator.

Perfect to restore facial structure and improve firmness and overall skin quality progressively and lastingly.

Dermapen

Controlled micro-needling therapy that induces collagen and elastin production. Helps topical products penetrate deeper.

Visibly improves texture, softens fine scars, minimizes pores, and boosts radiance.

OxiCura

Advanced facial that provides deep oxygenation from within. Exfoliates, oxygenates, and infuses essential nutrients.

Immediate glow with smoother, refreshed, and revitalized skin.

Ultra

Focused ultrasound technology that heats deeper skin layers, promoting neocollagenesis (new collagen formation).

Achieve a non-surgical lifting effect, improving firmness and redefining facial contours.

Skin Booster

Micro-injections of low-density hyaluronic acid that do not add volume, focusing instead on deep hydration.

Enhances elasticity, smooths the skin, and restores a healthy, dewy glow from within.
