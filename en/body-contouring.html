Body Contouring

Abdominoplasty (Tummy Tuck)

Also known as a lipectomy, recommended for post-pregnancy patients or those with significant weight loss to correct excess skin, fat, and abdominal musculature.

  • Toning of the abdominal wall.
  • Removal of excess skin, stretch marks, and fat.
  • The scar is normally hidden by underwear or a bikini.

Body Contouring

Liposuction

A surgical procedure focused on improving body contour by extracting fat from different areas of the body according to the patient's needs.

  • Common areas: back, abdomen, thighs, and arms.
  • Reduces sizes and achieves a more harmonious and aesthetic result.
  • 0.5 cm incisions to remove unwanted fat.

Fat Transfer (Liposculpture)

Also known as liposculpture. The fat tissue extracted via liposuction can be used to inject into other parts of the body, providing better contour and increasing volume.

  • Frequent areas: Buttock and hip augmentation.
  • The fat from liposuction is processed and repositioned.
  • 0.5 cm incisions to inject the fat.

Vaginal Rejuvenation

Focused on improving the appearance of the external genitalia, primarily by removing excess tissue and giving a more youthful look.

  • Ideal for post-pregnancy, hormonal, or aging changes.
  • Eliminates discomfort when wearing certain clothing.
  • Imperceptible incisions, without altering sensitivity.
