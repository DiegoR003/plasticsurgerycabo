Facial Surgery

Blepharoplasty (Eyelid Surgery)

It is the surgical procedure to remove excess skin and bags under the eyes, which can give the impression of tired eyes.

  • Rejuvenates the eyelids for a fresher, more youthful appearance.
  • The incisions are usually imperceptible.

Rhytidectomy (Facelift)

Also known as a facelift. The purpose of this surgery is to rejuvenate the natural beauty of the face, leaving the skin smoother and more youthful.

  • Repositions the muscles of the face and neck.
  • Removes excess skin, smoothing expression lines.
  • Natural and rejuvenated result, without changing the expression.

Otoplasty (Ear Surgery)

It is the surgical procedure focused on improving the aesthetics of the ear or ears, especially when they are prominent or poorly defined.

  • Improves the angle between the ear and the head.
  • The incision is practically invisible as it is made on the back of the ear.

Bichectomy (Buccal Fat Removal)

The purpose of this surgical procedure is to remove the buccal fat pads, which are located in the cheeks. This can slim the appearance of the face.

  • Improves the appearance of the cheekbone and mandibular border.
  • Surgery under local anesthesia with a small incision inside the mouth.

Facial Surgery

Mentoplasty (Chin Surgery)

This surgical procedure aims to give greater projection to the chin, improving and harmonizing the facial profile.

  • A silicone implant with a texture and hardness similar to bone is placed.
  • It is performed through a small incision under the chin.
