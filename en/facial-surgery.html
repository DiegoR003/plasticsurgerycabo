Facial Surgery

Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery)

It is the surgical procedure to remove excess skin and bags under the eyes; that can give the impression of a tired eye. The goal is to rejuvenate the eyelids for a fresher and more youthful appearance The incisions are usually imperceptible.

Rhytidectomy

Also known as Face Lift. The purpose of this surgery is to rejuvenate the natural beauty of the face leaving a smoother and more youthful skin. Facial skin is usually the first to show wrinkles and fine lines. Exposure to the sun, the stress of daily life and pollution can contribute to facial aging.

The technique consists of making incisions on natural folds of the face, so they are practically imperceptible, the musculature of the face and neck is repositioned, the excess of skin is eliminated, in order to soften the lines of expression obtaining a natural and rejuvenated result, without changing the expression of patients. This procedure can be combined with Blepharoplasty (eyelid surgery).

Otoplasty

It is the surgical procedure focused on improving the aesthetics of the ears, mainly when it is prominent or not well defined. This is achieved by shaping the natural folds of the ear or improving the angle between the ear and the head, the incision is practically invisible since it is performed in the back of the ear.

Bichatectomy

The purpose of this surgical procedure is to eliminate the fat bags of bichat, which are found on the cheeks. This can sharpen the appearance of the face, improving the appearance of the cheekbone and the mandibular margin. The surgery is usuall.

Mentoplasty

This surgical procedure aims to give a greater projection to the chin, improving and giving harmony to the profile of the face. It is performed by a small incision below the chin by which a silicone implant with texture and hardness similar to bone is inserted and placed.
