Dr. Hector Cantú

Dr. Héctor Cantú graduated from the School of Medicine of the UANL, He specialized in General and Laparoscopic Surgery at the Christus Muguerza UDEM Hospital and received the sub-specialty of Aesthetic and Reconstructive Plastic Surgery at UNAM, at the Hospital Dr. Manuel Gea González and later in the Hospital Angeles del Pedregal, under the tutelage of Dr. Fernando Ortiz-Monasterio and Fernando Molina, one of the most respected surgeons in the area of Aesthetic and Reconstructive Plastic Surgery worldwide.
